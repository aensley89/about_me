Something about myself

Nationality

I'm half Malaysian and half Irish. My mother was born and raised in Malaysia where my father and her met. My father is originally from Michigan. I have never been to Ireland or Malaysia, but I hope to travel to both countries one day.

The official language of Malaysia is known as Bahasa. My mom tends to yell at me in her native tongue, but I don't understand what she's saying to me.

San Diego Living

As a child I always loved being around water whether I was at a lake or the beach. I make frequent visits to southern California visiting friends or just going for fun. I wish to live in San Diego once I graduate from school.

Sports

Sports are a big part of my life. I love watching basically any sport, but I enjoy watching college basketball the most. Ever since third grade the North Carolina Tar Heels have been my favorite team, and yes it's because of Michael Jordan.

Some Things I Like To Do

One of my favorite things to do is wakeboard. I love being able to glide around on top of the water.

I enjoy having a good time with friends and family. Sometimes you just need to get a little wild.

When it used to snow during winter, and I couldn't be on the lake or at the beach snowboarding was the next best option. I mostly ride at Bear Valley because I have a cabin near the resort, but sometimes I enjoy heading up to Tahoe for the weekend.
